Everything about this event/website


This website is promoted by Spark arena and Spca.
- Spca works to make an environment which animals and humans live in harmony saving the animals that are in danger and assigning the animals a responsible and welcoming family.
- Spark Arena is a stadium provided by Spark, the owner of Spark Arena. They hold events and concerts for many famous individuals and groups giving the community entertainment.

We make this event through the help of Spca and Spark Arena to hope that we can promote harmony of pets and humans providing an enviroment where people can have fun entertained by pets. We hope from this event that people will take into consideration of adopting pets to reduce the amount of animals taken to the slaughter house.
